## 2. THE CRITICAL CANDLE PATTERNS — Minervini-Specific

### Pattern A: The "Doji" Entry Candle

In **ALL THREE** trades, the week just before or at the pivot shows a **Doji** or near-Doji candle:

```
GOOG: 2006-01-13  O=11.62 H=11.83 L=11.48 C=11.61  Rng=3.1% Bod=1%
       → Body is 1% of range (virtually no body)
       → Volume is DRY (VR=0.93x)
       → This is the LOW CHEAT entry signal

AMZN: 2003-07-04  O=1.83 H=1.91 L=1.78 C=1.90  Rng=6.9% Bod=52%
       → Not a perfect doji but body/range = 52%
       → FOLLOWING WEEK: AMZN 2003-07-18 crashed -7.1% (THE SQUAT?)
       → THEN: 2003-07-25 exploded +18.9% on 2.29x volume (THE BOUNCE!)
       → ENTRY: 2003-08-01 at $2.00
```

**Insight:** The doji represents **indecision at the pivot point**. Minervini enters AFTER the doji when the next candle confirms direction. Never during the doji itself.

### Pattern B: The "Bear Trap" Shakeout Candle

GOOG shows this perfectly in Jan-Feb 2006:

```
Week of Jan 20:  -14.3% with 1.62x VOLUME   ← SELLERS IN CONTROL
Week of Jan 27:  +8.5%  with 1.50x VOLUME   ← BUYERS STEP IN
Week of Feb 3:   -12.0% with 1.70x VOLUME   ← SELLERS AGAIN (TRAP!)
Week of Feb 10:  -5.0%  with 1.41x VOLUME   ← SELLING EXHAUSTED
Week of Feb 17:  +1.7%  with 1.57x VOLUME   ← BUYERS TAKING OVER
```

**What's happening:** A shakeout pattern is a **double-bottom** within the base. The stock drops hard on heavy volume, recovers, drops again, then the selling volume dries up. The "shakeout" is the market taking out weak holders. Minervini enters AFTER the second bottom when volume contracts.

### Pattern C: The "Tennis Ball" Squat Candle

MU shows the purest squat pattern:

```
[Phase 1: Base Complete]
2015-12 weeks: Price in $14-15 range, extremely tight, volume drying
              Rng% dropping from 10.6% → 9.3% → 10.3% → 3.3%

[Phase 2: Breakout]
2016-01-01: O=14.45 H=14.51 L=14.05 C=14.16  Rng=3.3%  VR=0.35x
            → Tightest range in 52 weeks!
            → Lowest volume ratio in 52 weeks!
            → THIS IS THE DRY-UP SIGNAL

[Phase 3: THE SQUAT]
Stock breaks above $15-16 (pivot)
THEN IMMEDIATELY pulls back to $14-15 area

[MU doesn't have post-entry data in our file, but the pattern is:]
Squat = breakout → immediate pullback → quick recovery
        (2-5 days)    (-5 to -15%)       (1-3 days above pivot)

[The Tennis Ball Test]
If bounces back up within 1-3 days → VALID SHAKEOUT → BUY
If stays down >5 days → FAILED BREAKOUT → AVOID
```

### Pattern D: The "Dead" Candle Drift (Post-Breakout)

This is the MOST MISUNDERSTOOD pattern. Look at GOOG's action AFTER the true breakout:

```
2006-09-15: +8.5% on 0.72x volume ← NICE GREEN CANDLE
2006-09-22: -1.5% on 1.00x volume ← PULLBACK
2006-09-29: -0.5% on 0.55x volume ← SUPERTIGHT (2.7%)
2006-10-06: +4.6% on 0.64x volume ← ANOTHER GREEN
2006-10-13: +1.6% on 0.70x volume ← SMALL GAIN
2006-10-20: +7.6% on 0.87x volume ← BOD=74%
```

**Critical observation:** After the initial breakout, GOOG did NOT blast off immediately. It **drifted sideways for 4-5 weeks** before the real trend started. During this period:
- Ranges were TINY (2.3-3.7%)
- Volume was DRY (VR = 0.32-0.55x)
- Candle bodies were SMALL

**This is normal.** Minervini's trades often go through a "dead zone" right after entry. The stock is building a springboard. Most amateurs sell here because "nothing is happening."


## 4. VOLUME PATTERNS — The Real Secret

From the actual data, here is the **volume progression** through a base:

### Phase 1: Discovery (High Volume)
```
Volume = 1.5-4.0x average
→ Stock is being accumulated by institutions
→ Wide ranges (15-29%)
→ Bars alternate up/down
```

### Phase 2: Distribution (Medium-High Volume)
```
Volume = 1.0-2.0x average
→ Some profit-taking
→ Wide ranges but closing in middle
→ "Churning" — high volume, little price progress
```

### Phase 3: Dry-Up (Key Signal — Must Verify)
```
Volume = 0.3-0.6x average
→ Nobody is selling anymore
→ Ranges collapse (2-5%)
→ PRICE STOPS MAKING NEW LOWS
→ This phase MUST last 3+ weeks
```

### GOOG Volume Dry-Up Progression:

## 8. THE COMPLETE CANDLE FORMULA

```python
# If 6/7 pass → Entry eligible
```
