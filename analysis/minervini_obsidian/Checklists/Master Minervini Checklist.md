# Master Minervini Checklist

## Market

- [ ] General market is supportive.
- [ ] Leading stocks are holding up or breaking out successfully.
- [ ] Avoid forcing trades during broad weakness.

## Stock

- [ ] [[Trend Template]] passes.
- [ ] Stock is in or entering [[Stage 2 Uptrend]].
- [ ] [[Relative Strength Leadership]] is visible.
- [ ] Volume confirms [[Volume Dry-Up and Accumulation]].

## Setup

- [ ] Base is constructive.
- [ ] [[Volatility Contraction Pattern]] or another tight setup is present.
- [ ] [[Pivot and Entry]] is clear.
- [ ] Entry is not extended.

## Risk

- [ ] Stop is known before entry.
- [ ] Position size follows [[Risk First]].
- [ ] Potential reward justifies the risk.
- [ ] Sell plan is written using [[Sell Rules and Failure Signals]].

## Review

- [ ] Attach chart image before entry.
- [ ] Attach chart image after outcome.
- [ ] Write what worked, what failed, and what rule changed.
