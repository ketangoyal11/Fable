## Book Examples

### AMGN — Trade Like a Stock Market Wizard p86

- **Pattern Subtype:** clean-stage-2-uptrend
- **Date Range:** 1992-1992
- **Entry Level:** Any pullback to 40-week MA during Stage 2
- **Stop Level:** Below 40-week MA
- **Outcome:** Multi-year Stage 2 advance
- **Key Lesson:** Stage 2 advance shows stair-step pattern of higher highs and higher lows. Accumulation signs: big volume on rallies, low volume on pullbacks. Stock may double or triple and still keep going.
- **Trend Template Score (replicated):** 7/8
- **VCP Quality:** constructive
- **20d Max Gain:** 1.46%
- **20d Max DD:** -20.0%
- **Replicated Chart:** [[replicated_charts/trade-like-a-stock-market-wizard-p086-amgn_replicated.png]]

> **Kimi Notes:** This is a teaching example of what Stage 2 looks like. The dashed trendlines show the stair-step nature. Volume pattern is classic accumulation. Key insight: by the time Stage 2 is underway, stock has often already doubled.
