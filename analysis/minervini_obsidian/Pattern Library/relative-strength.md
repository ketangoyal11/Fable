## Book Examples

### AMGN — Trade Like a Stock Market Wizard p190

- **Pattern Subtype:** rs-leadership-in-bear-market
- **Date Range:** 1990-1990
- **Entry Level:** Pullback to 50-day MA during market correction (~$1.50-1.75)
- **Stop Level:** Below the correction low or 50-day MA
- **Outcome:** Led the market higher when bull market resumed
- **Key Lesson:** During 1990 bear market, AMGN barely dropped below 50-day MA. As market declined, AMGN moved sideways and marked time, improving RS ranking. This is how a stock improves its RS during a correction.
- **Trend Template Score (replicated):** 8/8
- **VCP Quality:** constructive

- **20d Max DD:** -11.71%
