## Book Examples

### AMZN — Trade Like a Stock Market Wizard p182

- **Pattern Subtype:** rs-leadership-pivot
- **Date Range:** 2001-2003
- **Entry Level:** BUY POINT at pivot breakout above ~$22-25
- **Stop Level:** Below the pivot low or 200-day MA
- **Outcome:** +240% in 12 months
- **Key Lesson:** True market leaders bottom well ahead of the general market. AMZN gave a year to prepare for the buy point. RS vs Nasdaq Composite was turning up before the market.
- **Trend Template Score (replicated):** 3/8
- **VCP Quality:** loose

- **20d Max DD:** -23.94%
