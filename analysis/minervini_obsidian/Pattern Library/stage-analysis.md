## Book Examples

### CMG — Trade Like a Stock Market Wizard p104

- **Pattern Subtype:** stage-3-to-stage-4-transition
- **Date Range:** 2011-2012
- **Entry Level:** Short entry on Stage 4 breakdown (Minervini Short Alert 7-9-12)
- **Stop Level:** Above Stage 3 top
- **Outcome:** Down 23% in 3 days, 40% in 3 months
- **Key Lesson:** Stage 3 tops form after extended moves. Broker upgrades on broken stocks are traps. CMG was put on short alert 8 days before Citigroup upgrade. Stock fell 40% in 3 months.
- **Trend Template Score (replicated):** 8/8
- **VCP Quality:** loose
- **20d Max Gain:** 5.82%
- **20d Max DD:** -1.02%
- **Replicated Chart:** [[replicated_charts/trade-like-a-stock-market-wizard-p104-cmg_replicated.png]]

> **Kimi Notes:** Brilliant example of stage analysis in action. The broker upgrade was the ultimate contrarian signal - exactly when NOT to buy. Minervinis short alert timing was perfect. Note the volume spike on the breakdown.

### WTW — Trade Like a Stock Market Wizard p92

- **Pattern Subtype:** slow-stage-3-top
- **Date Range:** 2009-2012
- **Entry Level:** N/A - sell/avoid example
- **Stop Level:** Below Stage 3 top
- **Outcome:** Significant Stage 4 decline after extended top
- **Key Lesson:** WTW topped in May 2011 but took a year to build a Stage 3 top before transitioning to Stage 4. Some Stage 3 tops form slowly, giving time to exit. Others (like F5) form quickly.
- **Trend Template Score (replicated):** 6/8
- **VCP Quality:** constructive
- **20d Max Gain:** 8.42%
- **20d Max DD:** -0.63%
- **Replicated Chart:** [[replicated_charts/trade-like-a-stock-market-wizard-p092-wtw_replicated.png]]

> **Kimi Notes:** Contrast with F5 on same page: WTW gave a full YEAR to recognize the Stage 3 top and exit. Different stocks form tops at different speeds. The key is recognizing Stage 3 characteristics: flattening price, distribution volume, loss of momentum.
