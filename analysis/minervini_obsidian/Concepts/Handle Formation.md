# Handle Formation

Handle Formation is a tight pullback or consolidation that forms near the highs of a cup-shaped base, creating a lower-risk pivot point.

## Key Traits

- Forms in upper half of the cup
- Downward or sideways drift on light volume
- Tight price action
- Breakout occurs as price moves above the handle
