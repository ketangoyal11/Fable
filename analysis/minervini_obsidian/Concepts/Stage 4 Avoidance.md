# Stage 4 Avoidance

Stage 4 Avoidance is the discipline of never buying a stock that is in Stage 4 decline — below its 200-day moving average and making lower lows.

## Key Rule

> "By keeping away from this stock when it was trading below its 200-day moving average, you avoided a disastrous decline."

## Why Stage 4 Is Untouchable

- Price below 200-day = downtrend
- Lower lows and lower highs
- Institutions are selling, not buying
- The trend is your enemy
