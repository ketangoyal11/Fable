# Squat

A Squat is when a stock attempts to break out but briefly stalls or pulls back on the breakout day, before recovering and continuing higher.

## How to Handle

> "With no violations occurring and no stop hit — if you bought on the breakout/squat day — you stay with the trade."

## Squat vs Failed Breakout

| Squat | Failed Breakout |
|---|---|
| Brief stall, then recovery | Reversal with lower lows |
| Volume may spike temporarily | Volume expands on decline |
| No violation of danger point | Breaks below pivot/stop |
| Hold position | Sell immediately |
