# Long-Term Leader Comparison

Long-Term Leader Comparison studies the full lifecycle of former market leaders to understand how parabolic advances end and what signals precede collapse.

## Example

> "American Online (AOL) 1995-2005 and Radio Corp. of America (RCA) 1924-1933."

Both followed similar patterns: massive advance → climax top → 90%+ decline.
