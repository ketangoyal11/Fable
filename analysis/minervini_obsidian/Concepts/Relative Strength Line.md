# Relative Strength Line

The Relative Strength (RS) Line compares a stock's performance to a benchmark index.

## Key Signal

> "The RS line was in a long-term uptrend, moving into new high ground well in advance of the stock price."

## How to Read

- RS line rising = outperforming the market
- RS new highs before price = early leadership
- RS falling = underperforming
