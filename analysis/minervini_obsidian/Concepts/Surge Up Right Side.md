# Surge Up Right Side

Surge Up the Right Side is when a stock rallies sharply up the right side of a base on a big spike in volume, indicating strong demand returning.

## Key Point

A surge up the right side on volume is constructive, but the stock should then tighten near the highs before breaking out. A surge that keeps going without consolidation is often too extended.
