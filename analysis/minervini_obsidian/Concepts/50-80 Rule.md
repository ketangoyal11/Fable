# 50-80 Rule

The 50/80 Rule: when a leader tops, it typically declines 50-80% from its peak before finding a bottom.

## Key Insight

> "When Lumber Liquidators looked pricey the stock tripled, and when it appeared 'cheap' it fell 90 percent."

Never average down on a former leader.
