# Pyramiding

Pyramiding uses paper profits from winning trades to finance scaled-up positions while keeping dollar risk constant.

## How It Works

1. Start at 25% of intended size
2. After a win, increase to 50%
3. After another win, increase to 100%
4. Keep risk at 2:1 or better

## Key Rule

Never increase risk percentage — only position size. Only pyramid after a winning streak.
