# Overhead Supply

Overhead Supply is shares purchased at higher prices that are now underwater. As the stock approaches old highs, these shareholders sell to "get out even."

## How to Handle

Wait for the stock to consolidate near the old high with drying volume. The breakout above the old high on strong volume confirms supply has been cleared.
