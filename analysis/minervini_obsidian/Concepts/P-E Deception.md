# P-E Deception

P/E Deception is the trap of valuing a stock based on its P/E ratio alone, missing that high P/E stocks can keep rising and low P/E stocks can keep falling.

## Example

> "When Lumber Liquidators looked pricey the stock tripled, and when it appeared 'cheap' it fell 90 percent."

P/E is a barometer of sentiment, not a valuation tool for growth stocks.
