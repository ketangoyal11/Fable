# Top Competitor

A Top Competitor emerges as an old leader weakens. Leadership rotates from one stock to another within an industry.

## Example

> "Michael Kors (KORS) vs. Coach (COH) 2012-2014. A top competitor emerges as an old leader weakens."

## Key Insight

Leaders change as new companies innovate and old ones stagnate. Track RS to spot the rotation early.
