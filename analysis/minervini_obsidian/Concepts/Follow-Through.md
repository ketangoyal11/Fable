# Follow-Through

Follow-Through is the stock's ability to continue advancing after a breakout rather than stalling or reversing.

## Positive Follow-Through

- Stock up 7 of 8 days after breakout
- Pullbacks brief and met with support
- Closes near highs

## Failed Follow-Through

- Low volume on breakout
- Reverses within days
- Closes below pivot
