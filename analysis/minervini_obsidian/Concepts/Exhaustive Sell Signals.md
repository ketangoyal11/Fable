# Exhaustive Sell Signals

Exhaustive Sell Signals indicate a climax top and impending reversal.

## Classic Signals

1. Climax run — 50-100% in weeks on massive volume
2. Blow-off top — Parabolic move, huge volume spike
3. Giant weekly range — Largest of the advance
4. Close at weekly lows after big up week
5. Volume exhaustion on down day
