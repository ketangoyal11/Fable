# Failed Breakout

A Failed Breakout occurs when a stock breaks out but quickly reverses on increasing volume.

## Warning Signs

1. Low volume on breakout
2. Quick reversal on increased volume
3. Three+ consecutive lower lows
4. Close below 20-day line
5. Close below 50-day line

## The Rule of Lower Lows

> "Every consecutive lower low after the third becomes more and more ominous, and even much more so if volume is high."
