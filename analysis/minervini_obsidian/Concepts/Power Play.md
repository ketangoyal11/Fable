# Power Play

A Power Play is a high-conviction setup combining classic VCP characteristics, strong fundamentals, and relative strength.

## Example

> "Quality Systems, Inc. (QSII) 1995 — setup as a power play with classic VCP characteristics."

Power plays get full position size because the setup is technically sound, fundamentals confirm, risk is well-defined, and reward is asymmetrical.
