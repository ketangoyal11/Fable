# Supply Demand Dynamics

Supply/Demand Dynamics describe how a stock transitions from a state of active supply (selling) to supply exhaustion (dry-up) as it consolidates constructively.

## The Process

1. High volume decline — supply dominant
2. Volume decreases as base forms — supply diminishing
3. Lowest volume at tightest point — supply exhausted
4. Breakout on volume — demand takes over
