# Sell Rules and Failure Signals

## Core Idea

Selling rules keep small losses small and protect meaningful gains. The system watches for failed breakouts, breaks of key support, late-stage climax behavior, and distribution patterns that show leadership is changing character.

## Source Pages To Review

- [[Trade Like a Stock Market Wizard]]: candidate pages 311, 265, 316, 31, 225, 317, 58, 135, 193, 305
- [[Think and Trade Like a Champion]]: candidate pages 149, 175, 75, 27, 76, 148, 150, 165, 49, 145

## Checklist

- [ ] Exit quickly when the setup fails near the pivot.
- [ ] Protect gains when the stock becomes extended or violates support.
- [ ] Watch for heavy-volume reversals and exhaustion behavior.
- [ ] Avoid round-tripping a meaningful profit into a loss.
- [ ] Do not let hope replace the original trade thesis.

## Image Work

- Mark failed breakout bars and support violations.
- Annotate climax runs and high-volume reversal days.
- Compare exit trigger against original entry thesis.

## Scanner Mapping

- `Detect closes below pivot, 20 DMA, 50 DMA, or prior swing low depending on trade age.`
- `Flag extended moves with rapid multi-week gains and abnormal volume.`
