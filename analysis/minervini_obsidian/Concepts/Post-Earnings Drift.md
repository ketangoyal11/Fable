# Post-Earnings Drift

Post-Earnings Drift (PED) is the tendency of a stock to continue drifting in the direction of an earnings surprise for days or weeks after the announcement.

## How to Use It

1. Look for stocks with strong earnings surprises
2. Wait for the gap-up day
3. If the stock holds the gap and tightens, a secondary entry may form

## Caution

A gap up on huge volume that fails to hold is often an exhaustive move.
