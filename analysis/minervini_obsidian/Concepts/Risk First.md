# Risk First

## Core Idea

The system protects capital before chasing upside. Position size, stop location, and the decision to exit when wrong are defined before the trade, so no single idea can cause serious damage.

## Source Pages To Review

- [[Trade Like a Stock Market Wizard]]: candidate pages 310, 323, 317, 293, 314, 316, 311, 315, 343, 229
- [[Think and Trade Like a Champion]]: candidate pages 143, 26, 45, 43, 60, 164, 52, 59, 174, 44

## Checklist

- [ ] Know the exact price that invalidates the trade.
- [ ] Position size is based on account risk, not conviction.
- [ ] Losses are kept small and accepted quickly.
- [ ] Do not average down into a failed setup.
- [ ] Reduce size when execution quality or market conditions weaken.
