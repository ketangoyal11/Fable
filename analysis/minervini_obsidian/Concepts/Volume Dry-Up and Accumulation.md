# Volume Dry-Up and Accumulation

## Core Idea

Volume helps distinguish constructive tightening from weak inactivity. Strong advances with heavier participation and quiet pullbacks suggest demand; repeated heavy-volume breaks warn that supply remains overhead.

## Checklist

- [ ] Up days show stronger participation than down days.
- [ ] Pullbacks occur on lighter or declining volume.
- [ ] The final part of a base shows dry-up instead of heavy selling.
- [ ] Breakout volume confirms that demand is returning.
