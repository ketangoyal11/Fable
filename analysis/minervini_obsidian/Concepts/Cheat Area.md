# Cheat Area

The Cheat Area is an early entry zone just above a stock's recent consolidation but below the absolute high. It offers a lower-risk entry than waiting for new highs.

## How It Works

- The cheat area forms as the stock tightens near the upper portion of the base
- A handle may form just above the cheat area
- Both the cheat area and the handle provide viable pivot points
- The stop goes below the cheat area
