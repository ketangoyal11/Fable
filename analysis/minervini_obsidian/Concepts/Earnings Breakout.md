# Earnings Breakout

An Earnings Breakout occurs when a company's quarterly earnings break above the previous four quarters, signaling accelerating growth.

## How to Identify

- Current quarter EPS breaks above the trailing four-quarter range
- Two-quarter rolling average can smooth seasonal noise
- Confirmed by price action and volume
