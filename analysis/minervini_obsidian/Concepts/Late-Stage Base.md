# Late-Stage Base

A Late-Stage Base is a consolidation that forms after a stock has already advanced significantly and is nearing exhaustion. These bases have higher failure rates.

## Warning Signs

- Base forms after three+ prior bases
- Volume is erratic
- The base is wide and loose
- The stock is extended from its long-term moving average
