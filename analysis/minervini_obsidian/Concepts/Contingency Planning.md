# Contingency Planning

Contingency Planning is defining your trade priorities, loss limits, and profit-protection rules before entering a trade.

> "The important role contingency planning plays is that it enables you to make good decisions when you're under fire — when you need it the most."

## What to Plan

1. Entry price and pivot
2. Stop-loss level (danger point)
3. Position size
4. Profit-taking rules
5. Time stop
