# Price Maturation Cycle

The Price Maturation Cycle (base counting) gauges where a stock is within its Stage 2 advance by counting bases.

## Origin

Introduced to Minervini by Bill O'Neil and David Ryan.

## How It Works

- **First base** — Lowest risk, highest reward.
- **Second base** — Still constructive, risk increases.
- **Third+ base** — Late-stage. Higher failure risk.

> "By itself, base counting will not tell you if a stock has topped. It does, however, provide a great way to gain perspective on where you are within the stage 2 advance."
