# Low Cheat

A Low Cheat is an early entry before a stock fully completes its base and breaks to new highs.

## Characteristics

- Stock forms a base but hasn't broken to new highs
- Price tightens near upper portion of base
- Volume dries up
- Entry just above recent consolidation
- Stop below cheat pivot

## Examples

- Twitter (TWTR) 2015
- Apple Computer
