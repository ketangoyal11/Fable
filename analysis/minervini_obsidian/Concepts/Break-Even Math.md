# Break-Even Math

Break-Even Math shows how much gain is required to recover from losses. Large losses require exponentially larger gains to break even.

## Examples

| Loss | Gain Needed to Break Even |
|---|---|
| -10% | +11.1% |
| -20% | +25% |
| -33% | +50% |
| -50% | +100% |
| -80% | +400% |
