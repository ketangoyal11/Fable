# Trend Template

## Core Idea

A stock must already be acting like a leader before it becomes a serious candidate. The template filters for price above key long-term moving averages, constructive moving-average alignment, and proximity to highs instead of bargain-looking weakness.

## Source Pages To Review

- [[Trade Like a Stock Market Wizard]]: candidate pages 94, 83, 84, 89, 49, 87, 86, 90, 59, 78
- [[Think and Trade Like a Champion]]: candidate pages 105, 104, 107, 120, 108, 122, 133, 138, 106, 109

## Checklist

- [ ] Close is above 50 DMA, 150 DMA, and 200 DMA.
- [ ] 50 DMA is above the 150 DMA and 200 DMA.
- [ ] 200 DMA is rising or no longer declining.
- [ ] Price is closer to its 52-week high than to its 52-week low.
- [ ] The chart shows institutional demand rather than random recovery noise.

## Image Work

- Mark the 50, 150, and 200 DMA lines.
- Shade periods where price is below the 200 DMA.
- Add a small label when all template conditions pass.

## Scanner Mapping

- `close > sma50 > sma150 > sma200, with close > sma200.`
- `sma200_today > sma200_20_sessions_ago when enough history exists.`
- `close >= 0.75 * 252-session high and close >= 1.30 * 252-session low.`
