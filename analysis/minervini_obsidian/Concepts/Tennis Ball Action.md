# Tennis Ball Action

Tennis Ball Action is resilient price snapback after brief pullbacks — like a tennis ball dropped on a hard surface.

## Characteristics

- Follow-through after breakout
- More up days than down days
- More up weeks than down weeks
- Pullbacks are brief and shallow
