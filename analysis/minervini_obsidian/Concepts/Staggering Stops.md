# Staggering Stops

Staggering Stops splits your position: sell half at a tighter stop, half at a wider stop, maintaining average risk while reducing chance of full stop-out.

## Example

> "Sell half at 4% and half at 8% — maintained 6% risk, but stayed in half the position."
