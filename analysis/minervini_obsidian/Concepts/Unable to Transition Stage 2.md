# Unable to Transition Stage 2

Some stocks attempt to transition from Stage 1 to Stage 2 but fail, remaining in a choppy range or breaking down into Stage 4.

## Example

> "Novell (NOVL) 1994-1996 — was unable to transition into stage 2 and set up constructively."

## Why It Happens

- Weak fundamentals
- Lack of institutional sponsorship
- Overhead supply too heavy
- Wrong industry group
