# 3-C Pattern

The 3-C Pattern (3 Contraction Pattern) is a VCP with three distinct contraction phases.

> "Humana (HUM) 1978 — Stock emerged from 3-C pattern just days after the Dow made its low. The stock advanced 1,000 percent."
