# Massive Short-Term Gain

Massive Short-Term Gains are the outsized returns that SEPA setups can produce when all factors align.

## Examples

- WR Grace & Co. (GRA) 2004 — +147% in 55 days
- Rambus, Inc. 1997 — 150% in nine weeks, 1,450% in 37 months

## Key Lesson

When gains come this fast, they can disappear just as quickly. Have profit-protection rules ready.
