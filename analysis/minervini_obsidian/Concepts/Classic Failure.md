# Classic Failure

Classic Failure is the complete collapse of a stock after displaying multiple sell signals.

## Example

> "DryShips (DRYS) 2007. Stock displayed a plethora of classic signals. The stock subsequently declined by 99.8 percent."

This is why you must sell when signals appear — not hope for recovery.
