# Stage 2 Uptrend

## Core Idea

The preferred hunting ground is an advancing phase where price has emerged from a base, demand is persistent, and the stock is making higher highs and higher lows. The goal is to participate in leadership, not to predict bottoms.

## Checklist

- [ ] Prior base or consolidation is visible.
- [ ] Breakout has shifted the stock into a confirmed advance.
- [ ] Pullbacks are controlled and respect key moving averages.
- [ ] Volume behavior suggests accumulation on advances and lighter selling on declines.
