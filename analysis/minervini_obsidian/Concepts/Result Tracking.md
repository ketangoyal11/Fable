# Result Tracking

Result Tracking is the practice of recording and reviewing your actual trading performance month by month to maintain perspective and troubleshoot your approach.

## What to Track

- Monthly P&L
- Largest gain and loss per month
- Number of days gains/losses are held (Stubborn Trader indicators)
- Win rate and R/R ratio over time

> "Using a monthly tracker allows me to track my trading results month by month. This helps me keep perspective and troubleshoot my approach day by day."
