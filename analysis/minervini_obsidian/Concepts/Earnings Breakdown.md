# Earnings Breakdown

Earnings Breakdown is when a stock gaps down or breaks sharply on heavy volume after reporting disappointing earnings.

## Example

> "Opentable (OPEN) 2011 — breaks down on huge volume after an earnings report."

## Key Signal

The largest weekly price decline on the largest swell in volume indicates institutional distribution.
