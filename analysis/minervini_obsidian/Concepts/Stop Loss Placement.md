# Stop Loss Placement

Stop Loss Placement sets a stop that allows normal fluctuation but is close enough to the danger point that risk is mathematically controlled.

## The Goal

> "Allow the stock price enough room for normal fluctuation, but close enough to the danger point that's not too much risk mathematically."

## Placement Rules

- Below breakout pivot
- Below recent swing low
- Below key moving average the stock has respected

## Typical Ranges

- Tight setups: -4% to -6%
- Normal setups: -6% to -8%
- Extended setups: -8% to -10%
