# VCP Footprint

The VCP Footprint is Minervini's shorthand notation for characterizing a VCP.

## The Notation

Example: **40W 31/3 4T**
- **40W** — Base formed over 40 weeks
- **31/3** — Base depth 31%, final contraction 3%
- **4T** — 4 contraction phases
