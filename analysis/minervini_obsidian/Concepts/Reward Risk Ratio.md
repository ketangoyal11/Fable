# Reward Risk Ratio

Reward/Risk Ratio compares potential profit to potential loss on a trade.

## Comparison

- **Trader A**: Drops 20%, nails down 20% profit = **1:1 R/R**
- **Trader B**: Risks 5% for 20% return = **4:1 R/R**

> "Which trader do you think will be the bigger winner over many trades?"
