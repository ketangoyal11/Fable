# P/E Expansion

P/E Expansion is the phenomenon where a stock's price rises faster than its earnings, causing the P/E ratio to expand. High P/E stocks can keep going higher if earnings growth sustains.

## Key Insight

> "CKE Restaurants (CKR) 1995 — emerged into new high ground trading at a 55x earnings."
> "Apollo Group (APOL) 2001 — trading at 60x earnings; by 2004 the price advanced 2000%."

High P/E is not a sell signal if earnings are accelerating and the chart is in Stage 2.
