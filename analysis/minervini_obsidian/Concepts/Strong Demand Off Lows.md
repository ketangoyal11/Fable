# Strong Demand Off Lows

Strong Demand Off Lows is when a stock bounces sharply from lows on heavy volume before tightening into a constructive base.

## What It Shows

- Institutional accumulation at lower levels
- Buyers are willing to step in
- The decline was likely a shakeout
- Sets up a better pivot once tightening occurs
