# Price Shakeouts

Price Shakeouts are sharp brief drops designed to flush weak holders before a stock moves higher.

## Characteristics

- Quick sharp drop on moderate to heavy volume
- Price quickly recovers
- Often undercuts a prior low or moving average
- Weak holders panic and sell

## How to Handle

Do not panic sell into a shakeout. Check if your stop is violated. A shakeout that holds above the danger point is bullish.
