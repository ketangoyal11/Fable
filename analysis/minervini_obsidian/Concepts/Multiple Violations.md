# Multiple Violations

Multiple Violations occur when a stock breaks multiple support levels (20-day, 50-day, pivot) in succession — a major sell signal.

## Example

> "Breaking hard through the 20-day and the 50-day lines on big volume — a major sell signal. WATCH FOR MULTIPLE VIOLATIONS."
