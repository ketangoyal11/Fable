# Earnings Maturation Cycle

The Earnings Maturation Cycle describes how a stock's earnings growth and market reaction progress through a predictable life cycle.

## The Cycle

1. **Early EPS Build** — Earnings momentum builds gradually (10% → 30% → 50%). Smart money gets in early.
2. **Earnings Momentum Frenzy** — EPS growth accelerates. Expectations fuel a feeding frenzy.
3. **Price Momentum Kick-In** — Quant models buy purely on strong price trend.
4. **Growth Stock Status** — Growth becomes obvious. The stock is officially a "growth stock" in media.
5. **Smart Money Exit** — Early investors distribute while naive retail steps in.
6. **Stall & Decline** — EPS momentum lost. Negative surprise. Downward revisions.

## Key Insight

> "In order to find your next superperformer, look for stocks that are in stage 2 with strong earnings, positive surprises, and upward revised estimates."
