# Loss Adjustment Exercise

The Loss Adjustment Exercise is a mental tool to confront the reality of losses and commit to strict risk control.

> "Convincing Myself: The Loss Adjustment Exercise"

By quantifying what a large loss actually costs you in recovery time and compounding, you build the discipline to cut losses quickly.
