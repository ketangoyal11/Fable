# Earnings Deceleration

Earnings Deceleration is a warning sign that a company's growth is slowing. Even if the stock hasn't broken down yet, decelerating earnings often precede a top.

## Key Example

> "Green Mountain Coffee (GMCR) 2010-2012 — earnings didn't show material earnings deceleration..."

When earnings growth slows, the fuel for the advance is diminishing.
