# Stage 2 Requirement

The Stage 2 Requirement is the non-negotiable rule that a stock must be in a Stage 2 uptrend before you consider buying.

> "Insist that a stock be in a stage 2 uptrend before you consider buying. The proper time to begin buying is as the price emerges from a sound base or correct price consolidation."

## Why It Matters

- Stage 1 = accumulation, too early
- Stage 2 = advance, the right time
- Stage 3 = distribution, too late
- Stage 4 = decline, never buy
