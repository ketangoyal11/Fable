# Tight Consolidation

Tight Consolidation is a base where price oscillates in a narrow range for several weeks, indicating supply has been absorbed.

## Example

> "TASER Intl. (TASR) 2004 — emerged from a tight four-week consolidation."

Tight consolidations often lead to powerful breakouts because the selling pressure has been exhausted.
