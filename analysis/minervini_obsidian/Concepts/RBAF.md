# RBAF

RBAF (Result-Based Assumption Forecast) works backward from your goal to determine what win rate, R/R, and position size you need.

## How It Works

1. Define annual profit goal
2. Calculate needed trading opportunities
3. Determine required win rate and R/R
4. Adjust variables to make the math work
