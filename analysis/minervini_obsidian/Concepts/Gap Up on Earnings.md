# Gap Up on Earnings

Gap Up on Earnings is when a stock opens significantly higher after reporting strong earnings, creating a powerful entry signal.

## Example

> "Foster Wheeler (FWLT) 2007 — purchased on a gap fueled by a great earnings report."

## How to Trade

- Do not chase the gap blindly
- Wait for the stock to hold the gap and tighten
- A secondary entry may form as price consolidates above the gap
