# Bell Curve

The Bell Curve visualizes trading results. The ideal distribution is skewed right — many small winners, a few large winners, losses capped.

## The Wall

> "The minus-10 percent mark on my bell curve — The Wall. It's the largest loss I ever want to take. Not the average loss, the largest."

## Goal

- Keep most losses small
- Let winners run for right-tail outliers
- Never exceed maximum pain threshold
