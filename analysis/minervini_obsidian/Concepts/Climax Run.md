# Climax Run

A Climax Run is a parabolic advance where a stock rises 50-100%+ in a short period on massive volume, often signaling the final stage of a move.

## Examples

- TASER (TASR) — advanced 540% to reach all-time high, then advanced an additional 1800%
- Yahoo (YHOO) — advanced 170% then an additional 4300%

## What It Means

When a stock goes parabolic, the move is likely in its final stages. Smart money is distributing while the crowd is chasing.
