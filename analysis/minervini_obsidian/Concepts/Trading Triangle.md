# Trading Triangle

The Trading Triangle is the framework of three legs: batting average, gain/loss ratio, and position sizing.

## The Three Legs

1. **Batting Average** — Win percentage
2. **Gain/Loss Ratio** — Average winner vs average loser
3. **Position Sizing** — Capital per trade

## Benchmarks

| Batting Average | Optimal Gain/Loss |
|---|---|
| 40% | 20% / 10% (2:1) |
| 50% | 48% / 24% (2:1) |
