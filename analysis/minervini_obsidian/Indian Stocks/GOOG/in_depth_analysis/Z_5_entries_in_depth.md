## Lessons from Zillow (Z)

1. **Cup base breakout** — The Feb 2013 base had a $7-14 range with volume dry-up at the cup lip. The breakout came with explosive volume.

2. **Multiple bases on the same stock** — Zillow provided at least 4 distinct entry opportunities over 2013-2014 as it stair-stepped higher.

3. **"Up 7 of 8 days" follow-through** — After a proper base breakout, institutional accumulation creates consecutive up days with minimal pullbacks.

4. **The stock was "hard to buy"** — Feb 14 gap from $12.15 to $13.19 (+8.6% overnight) meant latecomers had no cheap entry.

5. **Earnings shakeouts as opportunity** — Both May and Aug earnings create temporary panic, which set up subsequent breakouts for those who watched the reaction.
