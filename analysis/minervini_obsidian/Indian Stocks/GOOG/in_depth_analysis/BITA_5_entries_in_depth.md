## The Classic VCP Pattern (Figure 6-5)

From the book: *"A classic VCP pattern that skyrocketed 465 percent in just 10 months."*

### VCP Structure (Reconstructed from Book Image)

The chart in Figure 6-5 (page 110) shows a textbook **Volatility Contraction Pattern** with:

| Phase | Character | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **C1 (First contraction)** | Deepest — wide price range | Initial decline from prior high |
| **C2 (Second contraction)** | Narrower — moderate range | Pullback after first bounce |
| **C3 (Third contraction)** | Tighter range still | Short-term consolidation |
| **C4 (Fourth contraction)** | Tightest — narrow range | **Final squeeze before breakout** |

### Key VCP Characteristics Visible in the Chart

1. **Symmetry of contractions:** Each subsequent contraction was narrower than the prior one
2. **Volume declining with each contraction:** As the pattern tightened, volume dried up
3. **Resistance at the top:** Each contraction touched a similar resistance level
4. **Support rising:** Each low was higher than the previous low

### Breakout from VCP

The breakout came on **expanding volume** — a clear signal that institutional accumulation was absorbing the remaining supply. After breakout, the stock ran from approximately the base resistance level to +465% in 10 months.

## Lessons from BITA

1. **Classic VCP** — BITA is described as a textbook example. The 4 contractions with declining volatility created the optimal setup.

2. **"Up 4 of 5 days" is the minimum** — Minervini looks for this as an indication that institutions are accumulating. 8 of 10 days is even stronger.

3. **Brief pullback = strength** — The 2-day pullback after the initial breakout was not a sign of weakness; it was a shakeout that allowed the stock to consolidate before exploding higher.

4. **Tennis ball action** — A stock that snaps back after brief pullbacks has institutional support. Weak stocks don't bounce back.

5. **465% in 10 months** — This magnitude of gain only happens with sustained institutional accumulation, strong fundamentals (Bitauto was the leading Chinese auto internet platform), and a proper base structure.

6. **Pattern symmetry matters** — The VCP in Figure 6-5 shows clear symmetry: each contraction is approximately half the width of the previous one. This symmetry is a hallmark of institutional accumulation patterns.
