3. **Sep 17 closed ABOVE the small consolidation range** (all closes from Sep 9-16 were $61.91-$64.58)
4. **Body% 99%** — confirms demand was aggressive
5. **Intraday tag of EMA10 ($61.39 vs $61.75)** was a shakeout — the close at $65.92 proves supply was absorbed

## Scale-In Concept (Entry 3 + 3B)

The combination of Entry 3 and Entry 3B demonstrates Minervini's **scale-in** technique:

```
Entry 3 (Sep 4):  $55.95 — Primary entry at 5-week VCP breakout
Entry 3B (Sep 17): $65.92 — Scale-in at EMA10 flag breakout

Combined Position:
  Avg cost: ($55.95 + $65.92) / 2 = ~$60.94
  Stop (Line a): $61.39 (below Sep 17 low)
  Risk at avg cost: $60.94 - $61.39 = -$0.45 (already profitable!)
  
  This means the scale-in worked because the primary entry was already 
  in profit when the scale-in was taken. The EMA10 flag was the "rest area" 
  where a secondary position could be added.
```

**Key rule:** Scale-ins should only be taken when the primary position is already showing profit. The EMA10 flag provided that opportunity.
