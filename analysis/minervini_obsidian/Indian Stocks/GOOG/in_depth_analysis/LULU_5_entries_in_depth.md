## The Double Bottom Base (Jul → Nov 2010)

**Base Type:** Double Bottom with VCP tightening
**Base Depth:** ~43% ($24.00 pivot to $15.54 low)
**Base Duration:** ~18 weeks (Jul → Nov 2010 — the double bottom legs)

### Critical: The Oct 8 Failed Breakout

Examine Oct 8: Open $22.72, High $24.00, Close $23.74 on 4.44M vol.
- This was a breakout above the $22.60 resistance
- **BUT it failed** — the very next day (Oct 11) gapped higher to $24.16 but closed RED at $23.16
- The stock then dropped for 6 consecutive days back to $21.55

**Why it failed:** The base was not tight enough yet. The right side of the double bottom hadn't finished forming. Volume on Oct 8 was only 4.44M — decent, but not the 5.72M that would come on Nov 5.

### The Two Bottoms — Clear Higher Low

- **Bottom 1 (Point A):** Aug 31 — Low $15.54, Close $16.43 (4.4M vol — climax)
- **Bottom 2 (Point B):** Oct 19 — Low $21.27, Close $21.55 (3.0M vol)

The second bottom at ~$21.27 was a full **37% higher** than the first ($15.54). This difference is unmistakable — the stock was under accumulation.

## Lessons from LULU

1. **The FIRST breakout can fail** — Oct 8 touched $24 but failed because the base wasn't tight enough. Wait for confirmation.

2. **Volume dry-up is the real signal** — Oct 28 and Nov 2-3 showed volume dropping to 0.84-1.0M, the lowest in 3 months. This was the signal the base was ready.

3. **Natural reaction pullback vs breakdown** — Nov 15-17 pulled back only ~3% to $23.35 on declining volume. This was a healthy shakeout, NOT a failed breakout. The difference is volume and depth.

4. **Buy the breakout, not the gap** — Nov 5 entry at $24.05 vs Dec 9 gap to $31.78. The early entry captured the entire move. The "second push" entry on Dec 8-9 still had room but with less gain.

5. **Multiple entry opportunities** — LULU gave three clear entry points: Nov 5 (breakout), Nov 15-17 (natural reaction pullback), Dec 8-9 (pivot breach + gap). The best entry was Nov 5.
