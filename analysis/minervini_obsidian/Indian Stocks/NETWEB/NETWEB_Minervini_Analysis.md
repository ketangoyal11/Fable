## LESSONS LEARNED

1. Entry on the breakout candle, NOT on continuation days
2. Tightness must be on the RIGHT SIDE of the pattern (while price stabilizes/slightly rises)
3. Tightness while price is STILL FALLING = wrong side (invalid)
4. Upper wicks forming while price rises = distribution (avoid)
5. Look for multiple touchpoints of resistance - that confirms the level matters
6. Scale-in opportunities are NOT full positions - they are pyramiding tools
7. Feb 18 VCP handle: Shakeout + coiling + spring + volume = Textbook!
