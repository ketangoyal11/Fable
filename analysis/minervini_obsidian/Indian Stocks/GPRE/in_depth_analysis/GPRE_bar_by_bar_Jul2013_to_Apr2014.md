## LESSONS LEARNED

1. **Let the base build fully** — This base took 4 months (Aug–Nov). Impatience would have killed.

2. **Volume tells the truth** — The quiet accumulation in Nov (low volume climb after the climax) was the real signal, not the noisy shakeouts.

3. **The natural reaction is healthy** — The 9-day pullback was NOT a failed breakout. It was the stock's way of testing before the real move. Selling here would have meant missing +80%.

4. **The tennis ball entry works** — After the first breakout and pullback, the return to new highs on massive volume (Jan 16) was a second valid entry.

5. **Stops must trail** — From $18.06 entry, the stop should have been raised to $19-20 after the Jan-Feb run, protecting the +25-40% gain.

6. **Climax = sell or tighten** — Mar 21 showed climax characteristics. Apr 2 peak followed by Apr 3-4 distribution was the exit signal.
