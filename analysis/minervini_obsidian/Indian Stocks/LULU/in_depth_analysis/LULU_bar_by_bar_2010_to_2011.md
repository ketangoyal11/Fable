## Key Minervini Lessons from LULU

1. **Double bottom with PED:** The double bottom base (Aug–Sep 2010) combined with post-earnings drift (Sep & Dec earnings) created a powerful one-two punch pattern.
2. **Stair-stepper advance:** After the breakout, the stock "stair-stepped higher providing only brief pullbacks" (Figure 10.45 caption) — classic institutional stair-stepping.
3. **Follow-through count wins:** The initial follow-through in Nov (up 7 of the first ~10 days post-breakout) signaled strong institutional accumulation.
4. **Tennis ball action:** Every pullback was brief and met with immediate buying — "hard to buy" behavior characteristic of big winners.
5. **Secondary pivot entry:** The Dec 6 pivot at $27.81 offered a second, more condensed entry point before the post-earnings gap.
6. **The peak:** The Jul 20, 2011 high of $64.49 with churning volume (5.6M but range narrowing) was a climax top — the final distribution phase began shortly after.
